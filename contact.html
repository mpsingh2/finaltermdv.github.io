Average suicides per 100K population recorded between 1979 and 2016.
Highlights:-

1. Finally we would like to understand how the suicide trends look like whether it is improving or getting worse. For analyzing we would calculate the median or average number of suicides per 100K.

2. Overall the average numbers of suicides per 100K for all countries have risen starting 1979 to early 2000 , with few sharp rises in between 1985 to 1989 and second spike in 1997 to 2000. Post year 2000, the overall numbers have stablized and reduced till 2015.

3. Russia, Japan have their average number of suicides reduced, while the Average number of cases for USA have stablized.
4. The overall reduction in the average of suicides per 100K of population can be attributed to:-
* Better Mental health management infrastructure/diagnosis/treatments.
* Improvement in hospital facilities/infrastructure.
* Nations formulating policies for better public physical and Mental Health.
* Enlightenment/Educational programs to help People understand the downside of ignoring mental health issues.
* Social education training programs conducted to ensure society does not treat patients with "Poor Mental heath" differntly and be more accomodating.
* Initiatives to ensure "Poor mental health/issues" are no longer a stigma.
Note: The trends are calculated based on the numbers reported to WHO. There are some years where the data might be missing or not be reported to WHO or the deaths might be incorrectly categorzied.In some countries suicides are considered a taboo or criminal offense by law.
